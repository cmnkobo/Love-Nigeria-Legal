Facebook Data Deletion Instructions – Lagos Love

Lagos Love uses Facebook Login to let you sign in securely. We do not post to Facebook on your behalf.

If you signed up or logged in to Lagos Love using Facebook and want to delete your data, please follow these steps:

A. Remove Lagos Love from your Facebook account

  1. Go to your Facebook Account's Settings & Privacy and click Settings.
  2. In the sidebar, click Apps and Websites.
  3. Find Lagos Love in the list of connected apps and click it.
  4. Click Remove to revoke Lagos Love's access to your Facebook account.

B. Request deletion of your Lagos Love account data

After removing the app from Facebook, please contact us so we can delete your Lagos Love account data (profile, photos, messages, and other personal information) from our servers:

  • Send an email to providencechris_11@gmail.com with the subject "Lagos Love – Data Deletion Request".
  • Include the email address or phone number associated with your Lagos Love account.

We will delete your account and associated personal data within 30 days of receiving your request and will confirm by email once deletion is complete.
