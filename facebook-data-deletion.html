Facebook Data Deletion Instructions – Love Nigeria

Love Nigeria uses Facebook Login to let you sign in securely. We do not post to Facebook on your behalf.

If you signed up or logged in to Love Nigeria using Facebook and want to delete your data, please follow these steps:

A. Remove Love Nigeria from your Facebook account

  1. Go to your Facebook Account's Settings & Privacy and click Settings.
  2. In the sidebar, click Apps and Websites.
  3. Find Love Nigeria in the list of connected apps and click it.
  4. Click Remove to revoke Love Nigeria's access to your Facebook account.

B. Request deletion of your Love Nigeria account data

After removing the app from Facebook, please contact us so we can delete your Love Nigeria account data (profile, photos, messages, and other personal information) from our servers:

  • Send an email to providencechris_11@gmail.com with the subject "Love Nigeria – Data Deletion Request".
  • Include the email address or phone number associated with your Love Nigeria account.

We will delete your account and associated personal data within 30 days of receiving your request and will confirm by email once deletion is complete.
