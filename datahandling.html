What Data We Collect
Collection

We only collect the minimum information needed to provide you with a safe and personalized dating experience.

  • Account details: Name, email address, phone number, date of birth, gender, and securely hashed password.
  • Profile information: Photos, bio, interests, location (city/region), and matching preferences.
  • Usage data: App interactions, likes, matches, messages, and basic device info (device model, OS version) for security and analytics.
  • Third-party sign-in: If you use Facebook or Google login, we may receive your name, email, profile picture, and unique ID. We never post to your social media.
2
How We Use Your Data
Usage

Your information powers the core features that help you connect with compatible people.

  • Creating and managing your account and profile
  • Showing you compatible matches based on preferences and location
  • Enabling chat, likes, matches, and other interactions
  • Protecting users by detecting spam, fraud, and abuse
  • Improving the app using anonymous or aggregated statistics
3
Storage & Protection
Security

We use industry-leading security measures to protect your information.

  • Data is stored on secure cloud servers (Firebase/Google Cloud)
  • Passwords and sensitive data are encrypted or hashed
  • Access is restricted to authorized personnel only
  • Regular security audits and updates

While no system is 100% secure, we continuously work to keep your data safe using best practices and modern security standards.

4
Third-Party Sharing
Sharing

We never sell your personal data. Period.

  • Service providers: We may share limited data with companies that help with hosting, analytics, or payments, under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal requirements: We may share information when required by law or to protect user safety (e.g., valid legal requests).

Third-party providers can only use your data to provide services to us, not for their own purposes.
