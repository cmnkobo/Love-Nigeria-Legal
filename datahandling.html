How we handle your data

This page explains what we collect, how we use and protect it, and how you can access, update, or delete your information.

Privacy focused
Last updated: 2026
1 What data we collect
Collection

We only collect the minimum information needed to run the app and keep users safe.

  • Account details: name, email address, phone number, date of birth, gender, and password (stored in secure, hashed form where applicable).
  • Profile information: photos, bio, interests, location (city/region), and preferences used for matching.
  • Usage data: app interactions, likes, matches, messages, and basic device info (device model, OS version) for security and analytics.
  • Third‑party sign‑in: if you sign in with providers like Facebook or Google, we may receive your name, email, profile picture, and a unique login ID.

We do not post to any of your social media accounts.

2 How we use your data
Usage

Your information is used only to provide and improve the dating service.

  • Creating and managing your account and profile.
  • Showing compatible matches based on your profile, preferences, and location.
  • Enabling chat, likes, matches, and other in‑app interactions.
  • Protecting users by detecting spam, fraud, and abuse and enforcing our safety rules.
  • Improving the app using anonymous or aggregated statistics.
3 Storage & protection
Security

We use reputable services and safeguards to protect your information.

  • Data is stored on secure servers provided by trusted cloud providers (for example, Firebase / Google Cloud).
  • Passwords, tokens, and other sensitive data are stored in encrypted or hashed form where appropriate.
  • Access to personal data is restricted to authorized personnel and systems that need it to operate the service.

No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security, but we work hard to keep your data safe.

4 Sharing with third parties
Sharing

We do not sell your personal data.

  • Service providers: we may share limited information with companies that help with hosting, analytics, messaging, or payments, under strict confidentiality terms.
  • Legal & safety: we may share information when required by law or to protect our rights, other users, or the public (for example, responding to a valid legal request).

Any third‑party providers may only use your data to provide services to us, not for their own purposes.

Your control over your data

You can access, update, or delete your information directly inside the Love Nigeria app where possible.

Update your data

  • Edit profile information (photos, bio, interests, preferences) in your profile screen.

Delete content

  • Remove or change photos and other profile details at any time in your account settings.
Deleting your account

You can permanently delete your account from within the app (for example: Settings → Account → Delete account; wording may differ slightly).

When you delete your account

  • Your profile is no longer visible to other users.
  • We remove or anonymize personal data that identifies you, except where we must keep certain details to meet legal obligations, resolve disputes, or prevent abuse.
  • Some non‑identifying data (such as aggregated statistics) may remain for analytics and security.
Requesting data deletion by email

If you cannot delete your account in the app or want specific data removed, you can contact us directly.

Send an email titled

“Data Deletion Request – Love Nigeria”

Include

  • Full name
  • Registered email address and/or phone number
  • Username (if applicable)
  • A clear statement that you want your account and personal data deleted

Send to: your-support-email@example.com

After we confirm your identity, we will delete or anonymize your personal data within a reasonable time (usually within 30 days), unless the law requires us to keep it longer.

Changes to this notice

We may update this Data Handling and Deletion notice from time to time to reflect app or legal changes.

We will notify you in the app or by other appropriate means if there are major updates. The “last updated” date at the top will be revised when changes are made.
